My Passion

Why I Love Space

The universe is the greatest mystery ever encountered by human consciousness.

Infinite Scale

The observable universe spans 93 billion light-years. Every time I grasp that scale, my brain does a happy reboot. Nothing puts earthly problems in perspective quite like a Hubble deep-field photo.

🌌

Unsolved Mysteries

Dark matter makes up 27% of the universe and we have no idea what it is. Space is a canvas of unanswered questions that keep science honest and thrilling.

Human Ambition

From Apollo's lunar footprints to James Webb photographing galaxies 13 billion years ago — space exploration is humanity at its most courageous and creative.
